Contacts 5+ Privacy Policy

Personal Information Accessed

Contacts 5+ accesses the following information about its users:

  • Google account names
  • Contacts associated with accounts

Handling of Personal Information

Contacts 5+ does not store, monitor, retransmit, or do anything with the information it accesses, other than provide it to the user within the application.

Advertising

Contacts 5+ uses Google's Admob advertising service. Any privacy issues related to Admob apply to this application. Contacts 5+ has no involvement with Admob beyond the most basic implementation.

Closing

Contacts 5+ was written mostly for the enjoyment of its creator, and posted on the Play Store for the benefit of anyone else who might be interested. There is no intention of collecting user data for any purpose whatsoever. Users can feel safe that the creator of Contacts 5+ has no interest whatsoever in their data.
