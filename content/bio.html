Biography

Background

Craig Miller is a year old software developer living and working in Tampa, Florida. He brings a detail-focused, object-oriented approach to development, and has experience with multiple languages. His training came from a combination of online courses, self teaching, and a passion for developing software on his own.

At a young age, Craig's family strongly supported and encouraged his development work. Both his father and uncle had achieved a great deal of success as software developers, and they instilled in him from a young age a passion for technology. While Craig didn't pursue Computer Science in College, after graduation he discovered that was where his passion truly lay.

Today, Craig works as a Java Developer for a Tampa-based firm. Outside of work, however, he continues to expand his skillset with a wide variety of personal projects in various languages.

Academics

While in school, Craig pursued his passion in other fields, all the while engaging with technology as a hobby.
