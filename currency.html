Money and Currency Conversion

What started as my first experiment with accessing a web based service and retrieving information transformed into a pretty awesome little library. It ties BigDecimal values to currency types, which enables a number of special operations. Essentially it allows amounts of money of various currency types to be compared, combined, and otherwise operated on by a program.

Full JavaDoc: Money and Currency Conversion JavaDoc

Key Components

Operations, Regardless of Currency Type

The raw value of an amount of money is completely tied to its currency type, and the type can't be changed without converting the value as well. This conversion mechanism is used constantly to ensure consistent operations between Money instances, regardless of currency type. Add, subtract, multiply, divide, and even compare operations produce consistent results using the current conversion rate definitions.

Up to Date Conversion Rates for 24 Currencies

Currency conversion rates are downloaded from Yahoo! Finance, and 24 currencies are currently supported. However, so long as the enum constant adheres to the ISO4217 standard, there is no limit on the number of currency types that could be easily added, simply by adding them to the enum.
